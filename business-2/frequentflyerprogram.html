Frequent Flyer Program Info

At Southern Breeze Airways, we deeply appreciate our customers. As a token of our appreciation, we will always match half the miles you fly when you become a certifed Frequent Flyer, or when you fly mor than 6000 miles a year.

There is no registration required for the program at this time. For now, you will be issued a unique code to use whenever booking with us.
